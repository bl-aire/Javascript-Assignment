Quadratic Equation

The last time I was in contact with Quadratic Equation was during my WAEC. I studied a paramedic course and had nothing to do with Mathematics for years. To solve the problem, I studied to understand how Quadratic Equation works again. And on studying, I came to the conclusion that my Javascript code had to:

  • Accept arguements/ have arguements passed into it so I can test the equation. Hence the use of a Function with the variables a, b and c passed into it.
  • Make provision for two results to fit the +- operation after b2
  • Make provision for when the discriminant(equation under the square root) is 0, less than 0 or greater than 0. And for this, I used conditional statements.
